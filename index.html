PicMe.

A marketplace connecting spectators at sporting events and their photographs with participants.

ABOUT

Although professional photographers are present at most mass sporting events, we have been constantly disappointed by the cost and lack of variety of such pictures. The photographers tend to stay put in one place so the chances of getting a great candid action shot are slim.

PicMe transforms the hundreds, even thousands, of spectators into event photographers.

While standing at the sidelines, spectators can snap action photos, tag the subject with their bib number and upload to the app. The participant is then able to download the tagged photographs from the app for a fee.

  • SNAP
  • TAG
  • UPLOAD

It's as easy as that.

Why PicMe?

  1. Low-cost, high-quality and personal pics
  2. More shots, greater variety and better action pics
  3. Get paid to take photos
