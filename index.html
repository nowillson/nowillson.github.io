ABOUT

Professional photographers are present at most mass sporting events, but we have constantly been disappointed by the cost and lack of variety of their pictures. The photographers tend to stay put in one place so the chances of getting a great candid action shot are slim.

PicMe transforms the hundreds, even thousands, of spectators into event photographers.
